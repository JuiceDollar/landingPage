What is JUSD Lending?

JUSD lending is a decentralized function on the Citrea Layer-2 that allows you to borrow JUSD (the ecosystem's stablecoin) by using your Bitcoin (cBTC) as collateral. This way, you can unlock liquidity.

How does the lending process work?

• Enter the desired amount of BTC collateral
• Define your liquidation price and expiration date.  
• Once everything is configured, the JUSD will be sent directly to your wallet.

What can I do with the borrowed cUSD?

With the JUSD you borrow, you can:

• Hold JUSD, 
• Swap JUSD for another asset, 
• Withdraw JUSD to your bank account, 
• Use JUSD in daily life.

What happens if the liquidation price is reached?

If the liquidation price is reached, your position will be liquidated, and the deposited asset will be auctioned off. The collateral reserve goes to the bidders and the protocol as profit. It’s important to carefully choose your liquidation price to avoid unwanted liquidations.

What happens if I exceed the expiration date?

If you exceed the expiration date, your collateral will be auctioned off. It’s important to act in time and repay or extend the loan amount to avoid unwanted liquidations. 

How long does it take to receive my borrowed cUSD?

Once you’ve completed all steps, the borrowed cUSD will be instantly sent to your wallet.
